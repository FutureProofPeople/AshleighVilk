About Ashleigh Vilk

Ashleigh Vilk is the Head of Education at the Self-worth Academy, dedicated to empowering young students and school staff to navigate the challenges of an unpredictable world. With a background in business consulting and counseling, she is passionate about safeguarding the mental health of future generations.

Keynote: The Power of Self-Worth

Ashleigh's keynote focuses on how building self-worth can enhance intrinsic motivation, increase resilience, and improve overall well-being. By shifting perspectives from self-esteem to self-worth, attendees will learn to embrace their identity and cultivate a culture of kindness. This engaging session is designed to equip students and educators with the tools needed to thrive amidst the pressures of modern life.
